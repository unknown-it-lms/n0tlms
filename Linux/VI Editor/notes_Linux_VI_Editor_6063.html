Notes: Linux- VI Editor

Important commands
 
CommandDescription
iInsert at cursor(goes into insert mode)
aWrite after cursor(goes into insert mode)
AWrite at the end of the line (goes into insert mode)
ESCTerminate insert mode 
uundo the last change
Uundo all changes to the entire line.
oOpen a new line(goes into insert mode)
ddDelete line
3ddDelete 3 lines
DDelete contents of lines after the cursor
cDelete contents of a line after the cursor and insertnew text. Press ESC key to end insertion. 
dw Delete word
4dwDelete 4 words
CwChange word
xDelete character at the cursor
ROverwrite characters from cursor.
SSubstitute entire line and begin to insert at the begenning of the line.
~Change case of individual character.
